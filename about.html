Elijah Boisvert is an 18 year old musician from New Hampshire who enjoys creating music on piano, guitar, and ukulele, as well as singing. Elijah began playing the piano at a young age, and by the age of ten, he had released his first album containing ten self-arranged instrumental piano covers.

Under his previous moniker, Play The Keys, he released numerous instrumental collections and collaborative singles with other artists such as Joshua Tu.

In 2019, Elijah began including vocals into his performances, and released his first single, "Over the Moon" to public platforms under his current signature. Some of the biggest names that influence his music are Owl City, A R I Z O N A, Joshua Tu, Avicii, WALK THE MOON, Chillhop Records, Bastille, and many more.

Alongside of his releases, Elijah also enjoys performing for live audiences at both smaller events such as school programs, various churches, camps, and farmers markets, as well as larger venues such as the SNHU Arena in New Hampshire with Manchester Christian Church.

Elijah also enjoys creating YouTube videos for his channel that he started in 2016, co-hosting the podcast "Olio" with his younger brother Logan, as well as taking photos to share on the photography website Unsplash.

Elijah is currently working on his first album to be announced sometime during 2020. His education plans include studying Communication Arts, with a focus on Digital Media, at UNH Manchester.
